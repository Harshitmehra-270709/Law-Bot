Section 1: Application of Labor Laws in Uttar Pradesh
The Uttar Pradesh Labor Laws apply to all establishments employing 10 or more workers in the state.

Section 2: Working Hours
No worker shall be required to work for more than 8 hours in a day and 48 hours in a week.

Section 3: Minimum Wages
The minimum wages for different categories of workers shall be as notified by the State Government from time to time.

Section 4: Payment of Wages
Wages shall be paid before the expiry of the 7th day after the last day of the wage period.

Section 5: Leave Entitlement
Every worker shall be entitled to leave with wages at the rate of one day for every 20 days of work performed.
